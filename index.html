Hey, I’m Pavan Gaonkar! I developed an interest in physics despite the best efforts of my teachers. My curiosity about how the universe operates at its most fundamental level has led me to explore areas like quantum mechanics and general relativity (GTR). Although I haven’t studied GTR yet, I plan to dive into it soon.

Recently, I’ve also started developing a fascination for pure mathematics. What truly amazes me is how abstract mathematical concepts—ones that seem completely disconnected from reality, like linear algebra and differential geometry—end up having profound applications in physics. Take linear algebra, for instance. If you read Linear Algebra Done Right, you might wonder how such abstract ideas could ever be useful. But once you encounter quantum mechanics, you realize that without linear algebra, constructing quantum theory would be nearly impossible...
